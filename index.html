Conference Themes

AI Fundamentals in Drug R&D

Exploring the three fundamental elements of AI in pharmaceutical research and development, including data integrity, algorithmic transparency, and validation frameworks.

AI Applications in Drug Discovery

Examining cutting-edge applications of artificial intelligence in target identification, compound screening, and drug design optimization.

AI in Pharmaceutical Industry

Analysis of AI implementation across manufacturing, quality control, supply chain optimization, and post-market surveillance in pharma.

Challenges & Solutions

Addressing ethical concerns, regulatory hurdles, data privacy issues, and technical limitations in AI-driven drug development with potential solutions.
